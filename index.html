Bathroom Deep Clean

  • Showers, Baths, Basins, and Toilets: Scrubbed and sanitized
  • Water Outlets (Drains): Cleaned inside all water outlets (bath, basin, and shower)
  • Wall Tiles: Wiped down

Living/Common Areas

  • Dusting: Entire house dusted

  • Ceiling Fans/Air Conditioning Units: Cleaned where applicable
  • Floors: Thoroughly cleaned
  • Rooms: Thoroughly cleaned, including dusting, sweeping, vacuuming, and mopping
  • Fireplace: Cleaned
  • Entrances and Exits: Cleaned

Outdoor Areas

  • Braai: Cleaned
